1. Beginner's Course - Copper Foil Method

As a working stained-glass studio and a teaching facility, we offer a six-session, high-intensity, basic Stained Glass Beginner Course in the Copper Foil Method. Course tuition is £285.

This skill-based series of lessons introduces the student to the terminology, tools, supplies, procedures, and safety concerns essential to achieving basic competence at the beginner's level of the craft.

Included in the course are instructions on the following topics:

  • Glass Scoring & Breaking Skills
  • Cutting Patterns & Glass Pieces From Existing "Cartoons"
  • Grinding & Fitting Glass
  • Glass Project Construction
  • Copper Foiling
  • Soldering & Reinforcement Techniques
  • Basic Repair Methods
  • Cleaning & Applying "Patina"


2. Advanced Course - Hinged Box

A four-session course covering construction of a hinged box.

Course tuition is £285 and can take up to eight weeks to complete a single project.



3. Advanced Course - Stained Glass Lamp

A five-session course covering construction of a stained glass lamp.

Course tuition is £245 and can take up to eight weeks to complete a single project.



4. Advanced Course - Kaleidoscope

A five-session course covering construction of a kaleidoscope box.

Course tuition is £265 and can take up to eight weeks to complete a single project.
