 About Robinson Originals

Robinson was established in 2015 in Lancashire by the Robinsons. Mary Robinson gave a few stained glass gifts to co-workers and, literally, within hours we had our first order at giftshopexpress for 52 pieces. We became a licensed wholesale and retail business almost over night.

We design and create exquisite original bejeweled stained glass boxes, kaleidoscopes, mirrors, nightlights and “Plant Palaces” (terrariums), as well as window panels, cabinet doors and Tiffany reproduction lamp shades on Tiffany reproduction bronze bases.


Moving to London we found great admiration for our creations. ‘A Little Gallery’ represented us and owner Laura Fletcher wrote numerous newspaper articles about us and our stained glass work.

four years later we are still designers and fabricators of fine stained glass which will surely become the antiques of tomorrow.

all our designs are unique and original. Our work spans from contemporary to traditional, custom designs and Tiffany reproductions for residential as well as commercial clients, offering free estimates and consultations to plan your projects.

We have over 100 kinds, colors and textures of stained glass sheets, hundreds of imported colored jewels, bevels and bevel clusters, as well as the materials, metals, chemicals and tools needed to produce stained glass projects in our studio.

Always interested in teaching the art of stained glass we offer beginner and advanced classes in the copper foil method, carefully laid out in our brochure. Advanced courses include 3 dimensional projects, such as boxes and terrariums, lamp shade making and the construction of kaleidoscopes.

We also carry a complete line of tools and supplies for other stained glass crafters.

Please make an appointment to discuss your stained glass needs.
